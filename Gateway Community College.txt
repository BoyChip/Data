Gateway Community College is a public community college located in Connecticut. The college's campus is located in New Haven with an automotive technology facility in [North Haven]. As of February 17, 2010, Gateway had the highest credit enrollment among Connecticut's community colleges, with 7416 students enrolled in for-credit classes. It was formed in 1992 by the merger of South Central Community College in New Haven and the Greater New Haven State Technical College in North Haven.
