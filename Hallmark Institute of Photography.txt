The Hallmark Institute of Photography was a for-profit photography school located in Turners Falls, Massachusetts, operated by Premier Education Group. It is nationally accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges.[1] The school offered a 10-month certificate-granting program covering a mix of technical, artistic, and business aspects of photography.
The school was acquired by Premier after the bankruptcy of its previous owner.[2] and closed in October 2016 due to financial difficulties.
